Frequently Asked Questions

When enabled, the extension will automatically join giveaways based on your filter settings without requiring manual clicks. It will run at the specified interval and join up to the maximum number of giveaways per cycle.

Filters let you control which giveaways to join. The extension will check each giveaway against your filters and only join those that meet all criteria. Whitelisted games bypass some filters (cost, wishlist, groups) but still respect level requirements and blacklist.

Blacklisted keywords will cause the extension to skip any giveaway with those words in the title. Whitelisted keywords do the opposite - the extension will prioritize joining giveaways with those words, bypassing most other filters.

You can report bugs or request features on our GitHub Issues page.
